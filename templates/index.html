How It Works

1. Upload Scan

Select a 2D brain scan image from your device. Our system accepts common formats like JPG and PNG.

2. AI Processing

Your image is pre-processed and analyzed by three distinct deep learning models, each trained for a specific task.

3. View & Download

Review the visual results and AI-generated interpretations on the results page, then download a consolidated PDF report.

About The Technology

This platform utilizes U-Net based convolutional neural networks (CNNs), a state-of-the-art architecture for biomedical image segmentation. Each model has been trained on a curated dataset to recognize specific patterns and features within brain MRI scans.
