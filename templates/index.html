How It Works

1. Upload Scan

Enter patient details and select a 2D brain scan image from your device. Our system accepts JPG and PNG formats.

2. AI Processing

Your image is pre-processed and analyzed by distinct deep learning models, each trained for a specific task.

3. View & Download

Review the concise results on the web, then download a comprehensive, multi-page PDF report for detailed analysis.

About The Technology

This platform utilizes U-Net and other CNN architectures for biomedical image analysis. Each model has been trained on a curated dataset to recognize specific patterns and features within brain MRI scans.
